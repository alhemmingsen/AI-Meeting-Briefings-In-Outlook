# A-IMeeting-Briefings-In-Outlook
Generate briefing packages for your coming meetings in outlook via open AI or Claude

Here's the set up:
Tag certain calendar entries with the category: "Briefing Required" in your outlook calendar.
Then you can have Open AI or Claude do the background research on your coming meetings such as the backgrounds of all attendees and deliver you a meeting brief to your inbox at the beginning of the week.
